Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2025  ·  Contact: planetkawal@gmail.com

SpendDeck ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal finance application developed by Kawal. This Privacy Policy explains how SpendDeck handles information when you use our mobile application available on Google Play.

1. Summary

SpendDeck is designed with privacy as a core principle. All of your financial data — including cards, expenses, budgets, and settings — is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal or financial information on any server. There is no user account, no cloud sync, and no analytics tracking of your spending data.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

When you use SpendDeck, the following data is saved locally on your device using SQLite database storage:

  • Card details you enter (nickname, short label, last four digits, monthly limit, statement cycle, accent color)
  • Expense records (amount, merchant name, date, category, associated card)
  • Global budget settings and currency preference
  • Budget alert preferences (enabled/disabled)
  • Onboarding completion status

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or share it through your device's own sharing features.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

SpendDeck does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Payment card numbers or banking credentials
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or files from your device
  • Usage analytics tied to your spending behavior
  • Advertising identifiers

4. Notifications

SpendDeck may send local push notifications to alert you when you reach 80% or 90% of your global runway or individual card limits. These notifications are generated entirely on your device. Notification permissions are optional and can be revoked at any time through your device settings or within the app.

5. Third-Party Services

SpendDeck does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app does not connect to banks or financial institutions. A "Bank sync" feature is listed as coming soon and, when available, will be covered by an updated version of this policy.

When you download SpendDeck from Google Play, Google may collect information about your download and device as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This is outside our control.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device for as long as you keep the app installed. You can delete all app data at any time using the "Reset all data" option in Settings. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data.

7. Children's Privacy

SpendDeck is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us at planetkawal@gmail.com.

8. Data Safety (Google Play)

For Google Play's Data Safety section, SpendDeck declares the following:

  • Data collected: None transmitted off-device
  • Data shared: None
  • Data encrypted in transit: Not applicable (no data transmitted)
  • Data deletion: Users can delete all data via in-app reset or by uninstalling
  • Account required: No

9. Security

Your data is protected by your device's operating system security (device lock, encryption). We recommend keeping your device secured with a passcode or biometric lock. SpendDeck does not transmit data over the internet and therefore has no server-side security exposure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
