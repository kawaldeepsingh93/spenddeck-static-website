Features

Everything you need to stay in control

Stack your cards, watch your runway, and log expenses in seconds — all from one dark, focused dashboard.

💳

Card deck

Stack all your cards in one place. Track monthly limits, utilization, and days until statement close for every card.

🛫

Global runway

See total spend, remaining budget, and safe-to-spend daily. Know exactly how many days are left before you hit the wall.

📊

Monthly insights

Burn rate gauge, month-over-month comparison, and category breakdowns across Food, Transport, Tech, Bills, and more.

⚡

Quick capture

Log an expense in seconds — amount, card, merchant, date, and category. Haptic feedback confirms every entry.

🔔

Budget alerts

Get notified at 80% and 90% of your global runway or individual card limits so you can act before it's too late.

🔍

Transaction history

Search, filter by month, card, or category, and edit or delete any expense. Full visibility across your entire deck.

Screenshots

Built for clarity

Your data

Private by design

SpendDeck stores all your financial data locally on your device. No account, no cloud sync, no data sold.

📱

Local storage only

Cards, expenses, and settings are saved on your device using SQLite. Nothing is uploaded to our servers.

🔒

No account required

Start tracking immediately. There is no sign-up, login, or personal profile stored anywhere.

🚫

No data sold

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial information with third parties.

🔔

Optional notifications

Budget alerts use local notifications only. You can disable them at any time in Settings.
